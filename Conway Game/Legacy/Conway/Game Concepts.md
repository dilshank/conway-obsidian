# Concept

1. Simulated Stock Market (and listing)
2. Simulated Real Estate Market (buy, sell and develop)
3. Simulated Commodities Market (import, mining/harvesting, farming, manufacture, export)
4. Simulated Financial Market (interest rates, liquidity, etc.)
5. Simulated Consumer Market (consumer patterns change)
6. Simulated Population Market (people move, demogrpahics change, wealth changes)


### Resources

- Money (multi forex - USD, AUD, GBP, CAD, INR, JPY, RNB)
- Wealth Stores (gold, silver, gems, bitcoin, dogecoin)
- Shares (drives ownership in companies)
- Land (deeds to land)
- Buildings (need land right - lease or ownership, types of buildings, deconstruction, construction [new/upgrade])
- Energy (mostly state actor, but renewables open privately, supply only, storage later)
- Transportation (vehicles, public transit, import/export links)

### Economic Activities

- Mining (precious gemstones, graphite, sand, coal**, gravel, clay, rock, limestone) 
	Surface Mining, High-wall Mining, Underground Mining
	Ownership (land), Mining Rights, Environmental Impact, Finite Resource on Site
- Harvesting (wood, fishing)
- Farming (tea, spices[cinnamon, cardamom, clove], rice, fruits/vege, oilseed [sunflower, sesame], coconut, rubber), livestock)
- Refining (lumber )
- Tourism (hotels, attractions)
- Manufacturing (inputs, process, output)

### Non-state Actors

- Banks (savings, loans, mortgage)
- Insurance (policies, payouts)
- Energy Providers

### State Actors

- Central Bank
	- Money supply controls (by lending to Banks or issuing Bonds)
	- Interest rates (by lending to Banks)
	- Manages inflation (by changing interest rates)
- Regulators (industry)
- Customs Department
- Tax Department
- Legal Framework (creation of laws, voting body)
